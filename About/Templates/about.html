Who we are


Our Godhari Abascar Kandra is a team of young engineers and passionate teachers with the aim to make science interesting for school students and inculcate scientific temperament in children.

Our Belief


  • Making science interesting.
  • Easy explanation of concepts in simple and daily used language.
  • Children perform experiments/ activities to understand the concept better.
  • Making a working prototype inspires students for next-generation innovators.
